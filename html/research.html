    • CohesiveAR
  • An application that allows game developers to apply textures from the surrounding to virtual game objects to create a more cohesive and realistic environment.
  • A developer can utilize our program to procure the texture of a flat surface, then
    • Control the size and orientation for the patch of texture lifted
    • Modify/place virtual objects in scene with the procured texture
    • Allow physics interaction of game objects with users gestures
  • CohesiveAR builds upon previous work to extend texture mapping to AR objects, allowing elements of the physical world to be translated to virtual objects.

    Thermal Router
  • A fusion360 add-on that helps anyone, regardless of prior experience or expertise, design thermally sound devices. Example designs we identified as relevant and focused on included 3D printed watch cases, wearable heaters, and raspberry pi cases. A user can choose to either augment their design with conductive material, increase airflow through holes in chosen surfaces, or add on a customized silicone to disperse heat from sources that would otherwise be at risk of overheating.
  • My contributions included helping design the tool through many iterations, scripting significant portions of the addon in python after extensive research of the Fusion360 API, and running a large-scale user study + analyzing results.
  • Our paper is aimed for publication in UIST 2023.
